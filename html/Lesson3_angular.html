Routing and the Impact of Choices

One of the most important decisions to make in building a web application is how the routing will be managed. There are a variety of server and client methods to maintain state that enable single page development. The choice that I made here was one that was influenced by my desire to have multiple screen elements change based on the application's state. In hindsight, I believe that I could have done this better by not using the ui.router module, in favor of a dynamic directive.
